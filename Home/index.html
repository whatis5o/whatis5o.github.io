Kigali
A vibrant, hilly, and modernizing city serving as the nation's economic, cultural, and transport hub. It is known for its lush green, orderly streets, and bustling markets like Nyamirambo.
Musanze
A scenic town in northern Rwanda, known for its mountainous landscapes and proximity to the Virunga National Park. It is a gateway to exploring the region's rich biodiversity and cultural heritage.
Rubavu
Popular resort town with sandy beaches and palm-lined streets. It is less than an hour from the gorilla trekking base, making it a popular spot for relaxation after safaris.
Huye
Considered the intellectual and cultural heart of Rwanda. It is home to the National University of Rwanda and the Ethnographic Museum (National Museum of Rwanda).
Nyanza
The former, historic capital of the Rwandan Kingdom. It is known for its rich cultural history, specifically the King's Palace Museum (Nyanza Royal Palace).
