About AfriStay

Connecting Hearts to Rwanda

We believe every journey tells a story, and every stay should feel like home.

Our Story: Born from Passion

AfriStay was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to make authentic Rwandan experiences accessible to travelers from around the world while supporting local communities.

What started as a small collection of verified homes in Kigali has grown into Rwanda's most trusted platform for property rentals and vehicle services. We've helped thousands of travelers discover the Land of a Thousand Hills in the most genuine way possible.

Today, we're proud to partner with local hosts, property owners, and service providers who share our commitment to excellence, authenticity, and sustainable tourism that benefits everyone.

Our Mission

To empower travelers with seamless access to authentic Rwandan experiences while creating economic opportunities for local communities through verified, high-quality properties and services.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading platform for authentic travel experiences, setting the standard for trust, quality, and community-driven hospitality across the continent.
